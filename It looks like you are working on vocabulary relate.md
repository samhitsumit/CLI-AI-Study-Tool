**Here is a tip to help you get this question right:**

Think about the *purpose* of the place. Every location has a specific job:

*   **Cinema:** The job of a cinema is to show **movies**.
*   **Theatre:** The job of a theatre is to host **plays** (live performances).
*   **Stadium:** The job of a stadium is for large **sports events** (like football or concerts).
*   **Stage:** This is not a building itself; it is the actual **platform** *inside* the theatre where the acting happens.

**To remember it:** If the question is about *plays*, your mind should go straight to the word **theatre**.

When you are choosing an answer, ask yourself: "What is the primary activity that happens here?" If the activity is "watching a play," the venue must be a theatre! Keep practicing these associations, and you will master the vocabulary!
